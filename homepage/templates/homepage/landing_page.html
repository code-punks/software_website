Pulse Parking Management Systems

Our product “Parking Automation using IoT” is computer-implemented automatic system and method for managing Parking lot

FEATURES

Fully Automated

With Pulse Systems, parking lots are fully automated i.e. zero human intervention. This is achieved by the use of RFID reader connected to Raspbery pi which is connected to server.

Less waiting time

With all the tasks running fully automatic, parking lots achieve less waiting time for the people in the queue for parking. Digitalizing all the human intervention.

Security

Entry & Exit terminals of a parking lot are supervised by the camera & entry & exit is purely on the basis of authentication through RFID assigned to the customer. Illicit entries are denied.

Online Payment

Minimalizing human intervention has been the key factor of PULSE systems. All the payments related to a parking lot are digitalized. One can easily pay their dues online by accessing their dashboards from anywhere.
